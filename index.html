About the Game

Aurora Tile Story is an offline tile-matching puzzle game focused on smooth controls, clear goals, and satisfying chain reactions.

Swap tiles to create matches, clear objectives, and use strategy to maximize your score across increasingly challenging stages.

Features

  • Offline single-player puzzle gameplay
  • Simple drag-and-swap controls
  • Progressive level challenge
  • Clean, readable interface
  • Local progress and settings
