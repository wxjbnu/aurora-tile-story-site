Aurora Tile Story

Privacy Policy — Last updated: May 13, 2026

Aurora Tile Story is a fully offline single-player game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Aurora Tile Story does not require user registration, login, or personal information input. No data is sent to external servers.

2. No Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party services, including:

  • Advertising SDKs or ad networks
  • Analytics or tracking tools
  • Social media integrations
  • In-app purchase frameworks

3. Local Data Storage

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

4. Children's Privacy

Aurora Tile Story is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children or any other users because no information is collected at all.

5. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, the app relies on iOS device-level protections for locally stored game progress and settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

If the app's functionality changes in a future update, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please use the support link on the App Store product page.
